Tanzania Tourism Companies Dataset

Data Collection · Data Cleaning · Analysis · Excel

Independently researched and built a structured dataset of 50 tourism companies across Tanzania. Collected company names, locations, business types, service categories, package types, websites, emails, and phone numbers. Identified and flagged missing contact information, cleaned the dataset, and analysed key patterns to produce actionable insights about Tanzania's tourism industry.

Key Findings

  • 50 companies collected across 10 locations in Tanzania
  • Arusha dominates with 52% of all tourism companies
  • Budget & Mid-range packages are the most common offering (34.7%)
  • Identified 4 companies with missing phone numbers and 1 missing email
  • Safari is the leading business type across all locations
